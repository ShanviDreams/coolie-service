# Baggo – MVP Definition

## Minimum Viable Product (MVP)

The MVP of **Baggo** focuses on validating the core problem–solution fit: enabling passengers to pre-book licensed railway coolies at a single station and allowing coolies to receive and accept bookings digitally.

---

## MVP Goals

* Reduce passenger hassle related to luggage handling
* Provide coolies with predictable and organized work
* Test real-time booking and acceptance flow
* Ensure trust through verification and transparency

---

## MVP Scope

### Included Features

#### Passenger Side

* Passenger registration and login
* Booking form (Train No, Coach No, Platform No, Luggage Weight)
* Fixed price display
* Online payment
* View booking status (Pending / Confirmed)
* View assigned coolie details (Name, Photo, Coolie ID, Contact)

#### Coolie Side

* Coolie login (admin-verified)
* Availability toggle (Available / Unavailable)
* View booking requests
* Accept booking requests (first-come-first-serve)
* View passenger booking details

#### Admin Side

* Verify and approve coolie registrations
* View all bookings
* Monitor coolie availability
* Handle complaints and disputes

---

## Out of Scope (MVP)

* Multiple railway stations
* Mobile application
* GPS or live tracking
* Dynamic pricing
* Ratings and reviews
* Offline payments

---

## Assumptions

* All coolies are licensed and station-registered
* Coolies are familiar with train schedules
* Passengers have internet access before arrival

---

## MVP Success Criteria

* Successful completion of bookings
* Reduced passenger waiting time
* Increased coolie job acceptance rate
* Positive user feedback

---

## Future Scope (Post-MVP)

* Multi-station expansion
* Mobile apps (Android/iOS)
* Train delay integration
* Multilingual support
* Rating and feedback system
